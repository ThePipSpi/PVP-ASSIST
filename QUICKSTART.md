### Troubleshooting

**Problem**: Addon not loading  
**Solution**: Check AddOns folder name is `PVP-ASSIST`

**Problem**: Data not updating  
**Solution**: Click "Refresh" button or `/reload`

**Problem**: Can't find minimap button  
**Solution**: Use `/pvpassist` command instead
